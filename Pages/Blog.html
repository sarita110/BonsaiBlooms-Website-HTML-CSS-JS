About Technology

In this 21st century, technology has become a significant part of almost everyone. Since technology is ever on the phase of development, its progress has been nothing but incredible, no matter how vastly it changes. Technology means the skill to convey an idea to reach a goal. But nowadays, the term technology mainly signifies the vast

knowledge of tools, machines, techniques, crafts, systems, and organisation to solve a problem. It plays a significant role in modern society, influencing various aspects of our daily lives, such as the way we work, communicate, entertain, and educate ourselves. Technology continues to evolve rapidly, driven by advancements in scientific research and the increasing availability of data and computing power. We now have the ability to transform the environment, extend our lifetime, create big and interconnected societies and even explore various new things about the universe only because of these advanced technologies.


The advancement in technology has made our lives easier, more comfortable and enjoyable. It has reduced the effort and time required to complete a task, thus enhancing the quality and efficiency of work. It vastly made it easy to communicate and remain in touch with people from all around the world. Technology ... now touches all aspects of our lives, whether it is through mobile phones, kettles, kitchen microwaves, electric cookers, television, water heaters, remote control, fridge, or through other larger communication systems such as internet facilities, railways, air routes, and so on. Because of this ever advancing technology and their use in different fields like medical, industrial, banking, education and others, activities are now performed with more ease and effeciently. Thus, technology plays an extremely crucial role in the daily lives of human beings. Due to the pandemic caused by covid-19, the use of technology became more of a necessity for every individual, to maintain their private and professional life. Since people were confined to their home, the use of technology became varied and suddenly every major activites like studying in colleges, working in an office, business in market shifted to online platform making everyones life easier and risk-free. And even after the pandemic, the shift remained which is proving to be an asset to learn more efficiently for students, work remotely when necessary for professionals and spread business while providing services to large and far-away mass with much help of e-commerce, a powerful aspect of technology.

Even though technology is seen as a boon for easy and comfortable lifestlye, it comes with its own flaws.With technology, the efficiency of a task has increased tenfold and this is often the rationale why thetechnology could be ready to replace humans soon. A rise in technology goes to make sure that tasks ... that humans could do earlier can now be achieved by machines and technology. Thus, creating a shortage of jobs. The evolution of technology in our world today has created a dependence on our devices, tools, and processes. Over-reliance on technology can lead to addiction, particularly with social media and other internet-based platforms. People deal with different mental health problems, like insecurity, loneliness, underachievement about every little things about their life due to the constant comparision to other peoples best moments posted on social media. Excessive use of technology can lead to physical problems as well such as eye strain, neck and back pain, and poor posture. Especially children are at a great risk to become victim of cyber crimes as they are naive and get easily influenced by the unfiltered contents of different social medias. Although technology is already a major part of ones life, it is impossible to always remain upto date due to its vastness, so one must be selective about what to focus on.

People for a long time have been running businesses with a physical setup displaying the products and selling them locally through stores. But with the advancement of technology, doing business has been made easy with the help of e-commerce. So to sell our products, Bonsai Plants, we took this ... advantage and created a fully automated website called Bonsai Blooms. Because we made the whole process of managing the sells digital, we were able to run the business smoothly from our home and we were able to reach out to customers on globalscale as the products, their details and updates were present for all to see, save, buy or share at any time they wanted. And sinc e it didn't require high level of initial capital we were able to open the business confidently. This concept provided greater accessibility of products and services and most importantly flexibility so selling bonsai plants digitally with the help of technology made a perfect fit for team.

Overall Conclusion

Technology is an amazing asset for our daily life which has been developed and adopted because, among other things, it improves efficiency, convenience, communication, and access to information. It is also the driving force behind innovation, economic growth, and global development. Businesses are one of the most benefited ones from technology due to its global reach with minimal investment. So life is almost impossible without the help these technologies provide, but one should always be careful about its effect on both mental and physical so as to prevent oneself from over-reliance on technology. So, if you learn how to manage it properly, technology can be a great friend in the twenty-first century.
